How it Works

Our goal is to take the guesswork and stress out of protecting your valuable family footage for the future. A friendly VemoREEL staff will meet with you over the phone or in-person for a consultation. Next, we pick up your physical media and take it to our transfer center for safe and time-efficient digitization. Once complete, we return your physical media and provide you with a hard-drive and/or link containing the newly digitized media with easy-to-understand viewing instructions.
