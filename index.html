How it Works

Our goal is to take the guess-work and stress out of protecting your valuable family footage for the future in three easy steps.

Step 1: Estimate
Meet with a friendly VemoREEL staff member over the phone or in-person for a consultation. We’ll provide a thorough estimate for all your digital preservation needs based on media type and quantity.

Step 2: Relax
Sit back and relax. We’ll pick up your physical media and take it to our transfer center for safe and time-efficient digitization. Your precious memories are in careful, experienced hands!

Step 3: Enjoy
Once complete, we’ll return your physical media and provide you with a link containing the newly digitized media with easy-to-understand viewing instructions, so you can access your best moments on any screen.
